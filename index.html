Mobile Legends Bang Bang is a mobile multiplayer online game which is published by a Chinese developer called Moonton. Mobile Legends was released in 2016 and was mostly played by people in the Southeast region and ever since then gained popularity from various different regions. Mobile Legends is a free to play game which played on a ipad or a mobile device. The game is a skilled based type game and also a strategic game aswell. Ever since the game gained popularity, Mobile legends has done verioud different collavorations with different kinds of anime and games.
These collavorations are famous throughout the world and has gained alot of attention from the gaming community and since then gained alot of new players.
Some of these collavorationsare with, (JJk) Jujutsu Kaisen, (AOT) Attack on titan, One of the big 3 of anime, Naruto and many more collavorations

When playing the game, you and your teammates will be able to pick a hero and face againt 5 other players and fight in the Land Of Dawn. The main objectives in the game is to destroy the other players base and their turrets. In order to destroy the base you have to destroy the turrets frist. When killing the enemy heros you gain gold and experience points which allows you to buy items and level up your heros.
