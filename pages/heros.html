Heros

In Mobile legends there are 128 different heros with each having a unique ability, all those 128 heros have different roles some having 2 roles and some even 3.These 128 heros are divided into 6 different roles which are:
Fighter, Tank, Mage, Marksman, Assassin and Support. Each role has its own unique abilities and playstyle.

Roles and Team Roles

Tank

Tanks are the most durable heros other than the Fighter role. They tank the damage for the team as well as help the team give vision.
They are the ones who are usually in the front line of the team fight and they are the one who usually initiates the fight.
Tanks usually have a lot of (CC) Crowd Control skills which helps the team to catch the enemy and ensures kills.
The Tank Roles are played by the Roamers and even in the EXP lane.

Assassin

Assassins heros are the most skilled based heros in the game, they are not just heros you can use without any skill since you need to know when to go in and when to not go in Knowing when to go in and always having good positioning are the essence of being a good jungler.
Assassins heros deal a lot of damage and can easily infiltrate the enemy backline and kill the marksman and the mage.
Assassins are always played in the jungle role and always carry the spell Retribution in which they can secure the jungle creeps or objectives to secure the win.

Mage

Mage heros are the ones who stay at the backline and deals a lot of damage from a distance, some mages have a lot of (CC) crowd control skills while some having high burst damage skills and some may have both burst daamge and CC skills
Mages weakness is that they have low mobility and low hp and they are usually killed by assassins. but to compensate for this weakness is that they have high burst damage and cc skills.
Mages are usually played in the mid lane.

Fighter

Fighters are a mix of the tank's durability and an assassin's damage, but of course not as much durability or damage as a tank or assassin.
Fighters are usually at the front lines, zoning out the enemy heroes while also having incredible sustainability.
There are 2 types of fighters, there are some with great durability and some with great burst damage. since some fighters have high burst damage they can be also played in the jungle role.
but usually fighters are played in the EXP lane.

Marksman

Marksman are heros that deals a lot of damage from a distance like a mage but in physical damage, though there are some marksman that deals magic damage.
Marksman are really weak and deal low damage in th early game but in the mid to late game is where they hit their power spike and deal high damage.
Marksman are usually played in the gold lane and they are the ones who usually carry the game.

Support

Supports are heroes similar to tanks, but they are not as durable. Most supports have skills that provide shields or heals to the team.
Supports like tanks are played in the roam role.
