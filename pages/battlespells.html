Battle Spells

Battle Spells are special abilities that can be used in any game mode and are avaliable to all heroes.
Some gamemdoes in the game does not have all the battle spells avaiable to use such gameodes for example are brawl mode.
Battle spells are used in many different ways such as for offensive and defensive purposes.
