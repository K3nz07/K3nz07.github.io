Mobile legends is a 5v5 game which consist of heros items and skins.
Mobile legends consist of many different kinds of gamemodes, and each gamemodes are complety different from one Another.
Some of these gamemodes are made for players who wants to have fun, while some of these modes are for competitive players

GameModes


Classic

Classic is a Mode where you and your friends can play using the heros you have unlocked and their skills, Classic is a gamemode where a hero can be played by 2 players one in the enemy team and one in yours. A unique feature of classic is that you can que up no matter how many players you have in your lobby ranging from 1-5 players.

Rank

Rank Mode is the most played mode in mobile legends mainly, unlike classic where whether you win or lose you dont gain anything other than BP coins and exp. While in Rank games whether you win or lose you gain/ lose a star.
Stars determine your Rank and the more stars you have the higher your rank gets and the more rewards you get at the end of the season.
In Rank you can only que up as a solo, duo, trio or a squad of 5 players. And the higher your ranks get a banning system is implemented where your team aswell as the enemy team can ban a hero.
Ranks in MlBB ranges from Warrior, Elite, Master, Grandmaster, Epic, Legend and Mythic, Mythical Honor, Mythical Glory and Mythical Immortal

Arcade

Unlike classic and rank, Arcade is a gamemode where you and your friends can go into the land of dawn and play various different kinds of gamemodes and have fun.
Magic Chess and Brawl are some of the most played arcade gamemodes while also being a permanent gamemode in Arcade.
