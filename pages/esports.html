Mobile Legends: Bang Bang E-Sports

MLBB E-Sports

MLBB E-Sports is where you take your skills to the next level and comepting with the best of the best.
You can join tournaments and teams to compete for prize pools.

MPL

MPL is one of the biggest leagues in MLBB E-sports, it is held in different regions around the world.
MPL is a professional league where 8 of the best teams in the region compete for the title of MPL champions.
There are many regions in the world that have their own MPL such as:

  • Indonesia (MPL-ID)
  • Malaysia (MPL-MY)
  • Philippines (MPL-PH)
  • Myanmar
  • Singapore (MPL-SG)
  • Thailand
  • Brazil (MPL-BR)
  • Cambodia (MPL-KH)
  • Middle East (MPL-MENA)

MLBB M-Series

The M-Series is the World championship of MLBB E-Sports.
To qualify for the M-Series you have to be the champion of your MPL region. Some regions gets 2 slots so the Champion and the runner up gets to qualify for the M-Series.
The M-Series is a torunament held every year and is the battle of the best if not the best teams in the world.
To this date there has been 6 M-Series held and the 7th M-Series is coming soon.
The champions of the M-Series are:

  • M1 World Championship, Indonesia, EVOS Legends.
  • M2 World Championship, Philippines, Bren Esports.
  • M3 World Championship, Philippines, Blacklist International.
  • M4 World Championship, Philippines, Echo.
  • M5 World Championship, Philippines, AP Bren.
  • M6 World Championship, Philippines, Fnatic ONIC PH.
